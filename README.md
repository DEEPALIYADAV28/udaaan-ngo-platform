# Udaaan NGO Management Platform

A role-based web platform designed to digitally manage the operations of **Udaaan Aasma Tak**, an NGO focused on educating underprivileged students in nearby villages.

---

## Problem Statement

Udaaan Aasma Tak is an NGO founded in 2012 that works continuously with a nearby village to educate underprivileged students.  
Every academic year, volunteers, council members, and mentors change, while some members leave mid-year and others continue or return later.

Currently, most operations are handled manually, which creates several challenges:

- No centralized system for managing volunteers, council members, and alumni
- Difficulty tracking role changes across academic years
- Manual handling of class timetables and attendance
- Limited visibility for alumni and external supporters
- No structured platform for volunteer onboarding and exit tracking

---

## Solution
